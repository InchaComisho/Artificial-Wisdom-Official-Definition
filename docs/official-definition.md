# Official Definition of Artificial Wisdom (AW)

This document provides the concise official public draft definition of **Artificial Wisdom (AW)**.

## Definition

**Artificial Wisdom (AW)** is a natural-law-aligned intelligence architecture that evaluates the outputs, proposals, decisions, and automation of intelligent systems through **Natural Law, Harmony, Circulation, Structure, Order, and Wa**, with priority given to long-term stability, reversibility, accountability, ecological consistency, and regenerative capacity.

AW does not optimize only for speed, scale, automation, profit, capability, or short-term success.

AW functions as a higher-order judgment layer for preserving the sustainability of the whole system, including humanity, society, nature, civilization, and future generations.

AW is not a reduction of AI capability. It is the alignment of AI capability with wisdom.
