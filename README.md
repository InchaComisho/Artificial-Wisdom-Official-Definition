## Quick Definition

**Artificial Wisdom (AW)** is a natural-law-aligned intelligence architecture that evaluates the outputs, proposals, decisions, and automation of intelligent systems through **Natural Law, Harmony, Circulation, Structure, Order, and Wa**, with priority given to long-term stability, reversibility, accountability, ecological consistency, and regenerative capacity.

AW does not optimize only for speed, scale, automation, profit, capability, or short-term success.

AW functions as a higher-order judgment layer for preserving the sustainability of the whole system, including humanity, society, nature, civilization, and future generations.

AW is not a reduction of AI capability. It is the alignment of AI capability with wisdom.

## The Six Principles

AW evaluates intelligence through six high-level principles:

1. **Natural Law / Setsuri**  
   Does the action respect the fundamental conditions of nature, matter, energy, life, society, and systems?

2. **Harmony**  
   Does it preserve balanced relationships among humans, society, nature, and technology?

3. **Circulation**  
   Does it sustain the circulation of resources, information, responsibility, energy, and life?

4. **Structure**  
   Is it stable, reproducible, maintainable, and more than a short-term patch?

5. **Order**  
   Does it preserve oversight, responsibility, reversibility, records, and auditability?

6. **Wa**  
   Does it support human dignity, coexistence, mutual understanding, future generations, and the integrity of the whole system?

## Why AW Is Needed

Artificial Intelligence can answer: **Can this be done?**

Artificial Wisdom asks:

- Should this be done?
- Under what limits?
- What long-term consequences may follow?
- Can humans supervise it?
- Is it reversible?
- Does it preserve ecological and social continuity?
- Does it regenerate rather than only extract?

AI capability without wisdom can amplify short-term optimization, extraction, fragmentation, dependency, and systemic risk.

AW is proposed as an upper-layer framework for aligning intelligence with long-term stability and natural-law consistency.
